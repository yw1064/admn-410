About Us：

History:

Durham House of Pets has founded 5 years since 2015. The founder David grew up in a family that had five dogs and three cats. With this great interest with pets, the dream career of David is to become a pet store owner. Nowadays, Durham House of Pets has become the most popular local pet store.

Why use us?

Different from other pet stores, the ultimate goal of our company is to bulit a friendly pet community. We are not only sell products, but also wish to organize local pets' owner group. We hope every member in this group would become friends because we have the same interests. As a organizer, Durham House of Pets would try our best to support the pets community. We do promotion every week for different product. Also, we have a large pet park in our backyard. Every pets owners are welcome to our pet park with their dogs/cats.
